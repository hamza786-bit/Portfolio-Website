About us

we take care of your healthy life

E-Tabib Kare is a telemedicine company committed to empowering women by providing accessible and affordable healthcare. We are thrilled to announce the grand opening of our newest clinic in New Lyari on October 1st, 2024! This marks a significant milestone in our mission to bridge the healthcare gap in Pakistan.

Located at GS-2323, Block H, Phase-2, Gulshan-e-Surjani, Surjani 2, Siddique Goth, Karachi. Our clinic offers convenient access to expert healthcare providers through teleconsultations.
